Link to Project

Eat-Da-Burger!close

Eat-Da-Burger! is a fun app that lets users input the names of burgers they'd like to eat. Whenever a user submits a burger's name, your app will display the burger on the left side of the page -- waiting to be devoured. Each burger in the waiting area also has a Devour it! button. When the user clicks it, the burger will move to the right side of the page. Your app will store every burger in a database, whether devoured or not.

Technologies Used (NodeJS, Express, MySql, NPM, ORM, Handlebars, Heroku, CSS, HTML, Gitignore)
